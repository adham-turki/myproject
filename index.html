Financial Ratios Calculator

Analyze your company's financial health with comprehensive ratio calculations across all key categories

💰
Current Ratio (CR)
Shows whether company has enough current assets to cover current liabilities
Formula: Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities
⚡
Quick Ratio (QR)
Stricter test excluding inventory (which may be hard to liquidate)
Formula: (Current Assets - Inventory) ÷ Current Liabilities
🏛️
Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR)
Ensures financial institution has enough liquid assets for 30-day stress period
Formula: (High-Quality Liquid Assets ÷ Total Net Cash Outflows over 30 days) × 100
📊
Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR)
Ensures stable funding profile over one-year horizon
Formula: (Available Stable Funding ÷ Required Stable Funding) × 100
📈
Debt-to-Asset Ratio (D/A)
Shows what portion of company's assets are financed through debt
Formula: Total Debt ÷ Total Assets
🏦
Debt-to-Equity Ratio (D/E)
Measures leverage - how much debt is used relative to equity
Formula: Total Debt ÷ Total Equity
📈
Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR)
Shows how easily company can pay interest on outstanding debt
Formula: EBIT ÷ Interest Expense
🏦
Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR)
Ensures banks can absorb reasonable loss and protect depositors
Formula: (Tier 1 Capital + Tier 2 Capital) ÷ Risk-Weighted Assets
💯
Net Profit Margin (NPM)
Final profitability after all expenses, interest, and taxes
Formula: (Net Income ÷ Revenue) × 100
🎯
Return on Assets (ROA)
Measures how effectively company uses assets to generate profit
Formula: (Net Income ÷ Average Total Assets) × 100
💲
Return on Equity (ROE)
Measures return shareholders get on their investment
Formula: (Net Income ÷ Average Shareholders' Equity) × 100
🏦
Non-Performing Loan (NPL) Ratio
Credit risk associated with loan portfolio - loans not making scheduled payments
Formula: (Non-Performing Loans ÷ Total Loans) × 100
