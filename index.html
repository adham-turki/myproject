Financial Ratios Calculator

Analyze your company's financial health with comprehensive ratio calculations across all four key categories

💧
Current Ratio
Ability to pay short-term obligations
Formula: Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities
⚡
Quick Ratio
Ability to pay debts without selling inventory
Formula: (Current Assets - Inventory) ÷ Current Liabilities
💰
Cash Ratio
Most conservative liquidity measure
Formula: Cash & Cash Equivalents ÷ Current Liabilities
📦
Inventory Turnover
How quickly inventory is sold
Formula: Cost of Goods Sold ÷ Average Inventory
💳
Accounts Receivable Turnover
How quickly payments are collected
Formula: Net Credit Sales ÷ Average Accounts Receivable
🏪
Accounts Payable Turnover
How quickly suppliers are paid
Formula: COGS or Purchases ÷ Average Accounts Payable
🔄
Asset Turnover
How efficiently assets generate revenue
Formula: Net Sales ÷ Average Total Assets
⚖️
Debt-to-Equity Ratio
Financial leverage and risk
Formula: Total Liabilities ÷ Shareholders' Equity
📊
Debt Ratio
Percentage of assets financed by debt
Formula: Total Liabilities ÷ Total Assets
🏛️
Equity Ratio
Portion of assets funded by equity
Formula: Shareholders' Equity ÷ Total Assets
💎
Interest Coverage Ratio
Ability to pay interest expenses
Formula: EBIT ÷ Interest Expense
📈
Operating Profit Margin
Operational efficiency measure
Formula: (Operating Income ÷ Revenue) × 100
💯
Net Profit Margin
Final profitability after all expenses
Formula: (Net Income ÷ Revenue) × 100
🎯
Return on Assets (ROA)
How effectively assets generate profit
Formula: (Net Income ÷ Average Total Assets) × 100
👑
Return on Equity (ROE)
Return generated on shareholders' equity
Formula: (Net Income ÷ Average Shareholders' Equity) × 100
